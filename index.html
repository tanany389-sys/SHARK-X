Coral Reef Classics

We offer different reef sites—from mellow coral gardens for beginners to fish-packed pinnacles for photographers.

  • Depth: 5–18 m • Experience: All levels
  • Calm lagoons, bommies, clownfish neighborhoods
  • Perfect for long bottom time & bright photos

Plateau & Drop-off

Wide ledges leading to the blue edge—expect turtles, big schools, and a hint of adrenaline.

  • Depth: 10–30 m • Experience: AOW+
  • Great viz and dramatic scenery
  • Blue drop-off for that “flying” feeling

Hurghada Wrecks & Underwater Museum

We offer various wrecks in Hurghada — from photogenic hulls to the Underwater Museum with its iconic tanks.

  • Depth: 8–28 m • Experience: OW+ (site dependent)
  • Steel silhouettes, schooling fish, epic photo ops
  • Guides trained in safe wreck touring

Safari Trips & Special Dives

We offer various safari trips across the Red Sea and curated special dives in Safaga and Marsa Alam — think serene sea cow (dugong) encounters and thrilling shark dive sites.

  • Liveaboard routes tailored to season & experience
  • Special excursions: dugong meadows, shark hotspots, dolphin reefs
  • Small groups · safety-first briefings · pro photo tips

Private Boats & Islands

We provide normal and private boat trips that can include island stops, relaxed snorkeling, and guided diving for all levels. Build your perfect Red Sea day: quiet sandbars, coral lagoons, and sunset rides.

  • Custom itineraries: half-day, full-day, or sunset
  • Snorkel gear & dive guidance available
  • Private charters for families & small groups
